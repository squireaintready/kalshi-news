About Prediction Pulse

Prediction Pulse surfaces the news stories that people are actually putting money on. When real dollars are at stake, you get a clearer picture of what the world thinks will happen.

Why This Matters

Traditional news tells you what happened. Prediction markets tell you what people expect to happen—and they're willing to bet on it. We track these markets and translate them into readable stories about:

  • Politics — Elections, policy changes, appointments
  • Economy — Fed decisions, inflation, interest rates
  • World Events — Geopolitics, treaties, international relations
  • Tech & Culture — IPOs, product launches, industry shifts

How It Works

We continuously monitor prediction markets to find the stories with real momentum. Our AI analyzes the odds, trading volume, and price movements to write clear, conversational coverage of what's happening and why it matters.

What the Numbers Mean

When you see "73% YES" on a story, that means traders collectively believe there's a 73% chance that event will happen. The more money behind a prediction, the more confidence the crowd has.

Disclaimer

Prediction Pulse is for informational purposes only. This is not financial advice. Markets can be wrong, and predictions change as new information emerges. Always do your own research.
