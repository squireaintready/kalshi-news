About Prediction Pulse

Prediction Pulse automatically tracks trending prediction markets on Kalshi and generates conversational analysis articles about what's moving and why.

How It Works

Every hour, our system:

  1. Fetches the most active and interesting prediction markets from Kalshi
  2. Analyzes the current odds, volume, and market movement
  3. Generates a conversational breakdown of what's happening and what to watch

What Makes a Market "Trending"?

We score markets based on:

  • Volume — More trading means more interest
  • Uncertainty — Markets with odds between 20-80% are more interesting
  • Recent movement — Price swings indicate developing stories
  • Time to close — Markets about to resolve get priority
